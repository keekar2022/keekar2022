👋 About Me
I’m a passionate Cyber Security Architect with over 25 years of experience across IT, OT, ICS, IIoT and critical infrastructure environments, currently working as a Senior Security Architect at Adobe in Sydney. 

My work focuses on designing secure, scalable, and compliant systems that align with both business objectives and regulatory expectations. Over the years, I have led the development of enterprise security architectures, Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions, and compliance frameworks supporting large-scale digital transformation programmes. 

I specialise in bridging the gap between complex security requirements and practical implementation—building strategies, roadmaps, and governance models that enable organisations to operate securely while continuing to innovate. My experience includes strengthening security posture for critical utilities, designing IAM ecosystems, and evaluating emerging cybersecurity solutions through hands-on proof-of-concept and real-world deployment. 

Beyond my core responsibilities, I have a strong interest in advancing compliance automation and modern security governance using standards like OSCAL, as well as leveraging AI/LLM-driven approaches to transform assurance processes into continuous, transparent, and scalable systems.
I thrive in environments where innovation must coexist with governance, and I am driven by a simple belief: security should not be a blocker—it should be an enabler of trust, resilience, and business growth.
